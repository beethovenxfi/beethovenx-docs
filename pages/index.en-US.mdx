# Introduction 
![beets](https://github.com/defi-naly/images/blob/main/beets6.png?raw=true)

Beets is a global collective of contributors dedicated to developing open-source, decentralized financial software. With a strong emphasis on simplicity and usability, Beets aims to provide everyone with access to powerful, permissionless on-chain tools for capital preservation and growth. The Beets tech stack currently includes a decentralized exchange (DEX) enabling seamless asset trading and innovative yield generation and a Liquid Staking Token protocol, providing the easiest way for users to access native Sonic blockchain staking rewards.
